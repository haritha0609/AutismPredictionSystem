Supporting Your Child's Development

These recommendations are designed to encourage healthy development and should always be combined with professional medical guidance.

Speech Therapy

Speech therapy helps improve communication skills, language development and social interaction.

Occupational Therapy

Develops motor skills, sensory integration and independence in everyday activities.

Parent Guidance

Maintain routines, encourage communication and create a supportive learning environment.

Physical Activities

Regular exercise and play improve coordination, focus, confidence and overall well-being.

Healthy Nutrition

Provide a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, proteins and adequate hydration.

Professional Follow-up

Consult pediatricians, psychologists or developmental specialists whenever concerns persist.

Daily Development Tips

  • Maintain a consistent daily routine.
  • Encourage eye contact during communication.
  • Use simple and clear language.
  • Read stories together every day.
  • Limit excessive screen time.
  • Reward positive behaviours.
  • Promote interactive play activities.
  • Celebrate small achievements.

Medical Disclaimer

This application is intended only for preliminary autism screening. The wellness recommendations provided are general developmental suggestions and should never replace professional medical diagnosis, therapy or treatment.
